Writing, Notes

Welcome to my website! No fancy preperation of code or frameworks here, just a playful exploration of writing & my interests.

Thank you for stopping by. Most visitors that come here don't say much, but I appreciate you nonetheless :)

Ambition

  • To write for the joy of writing.
  • To read for the joy of reading.
  • To rest often, and appreciate life.

I would like to thank Joel Haver, for "Thanks for watching my film" and @dhrupadkarwa, for sharing his ambitions of learning and joy for his new Substack, Lit Visions.

Philosophy

Write clearly, with good form and pace, respecting others and aiding their understanding.

Don't make it perfect, or you'll burn out.

I'd like to help others manage their time while they engage with this site, so I will add a timer.
